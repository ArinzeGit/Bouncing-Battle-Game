How to Play

Player 1 Controls:

Use 'W' key to move paddle up

Use 'S' key to move paddle down

Player 2 Controls:

Use 'Arrow Up' key to move paddle up

Use 'Arrow Down' key to move paddle down

Game Rules:

Don't let the ball go without a hit!

If the ball passes your side without hitting your paddle, you lose 1 health point.

Hit the ball with your paddle to keep it in play.

Watch out for rotating obstacles!

Collect power-ups to temporarily double your paddle size.

The first player to lose all health points loses the game.
